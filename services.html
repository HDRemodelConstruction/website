Remodeling a kitchen enhance it apperearance and also it can increase your property’s value if you wish to sell it in the future.  At HD Remodel, we make the process of renovating and altering your kitchen’s design, style, and the overall feel both stress-free and mess-free. On top of that, the variety of aesthetic choices we give you is outright stunning… quartz, mosaic tiles, recycled glass, and, of course, granite. All are great products for remodeling countertops and backsplashes.

You can also pick from a wide selection of either wood or laminate products to reface your cabinets. We have everything you need to turn your kitchen into everything you want, and need it to be!

Basement Finishing & Remodel

Basement finishing offers a bonus for homeowners looking to increase their living space and can be the perfect solution for growing families.  Once renovated, it can be a perfect hangout for kids and adults, too. Basement finishing can turn that dark, unused storage space into much needed extra square footage for a lounge area, exercise room, children’s playroom, guest suite, sound proof area for a home theater, or even a wine cellar.

Basement finishing offers a bonus for homeowners looking to increase their living space. But unlike the rest of the house, these below-grade rooms require extra planning and prep work. We take the time to understand your requirements and translate them into a building plan that we use to create your new living space.
